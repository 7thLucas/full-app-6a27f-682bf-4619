Foundation Validated

Generic CVs and unfocused interview prep waste time and reduce interview conversion rates

The Problem

Job seekers submit generic CVs that miss ATS keywords and specific role requirements, then scramble to prepare for interviews using disconnected tools. The result: lower interview conversion rates and wasted preparation effort.

The Solution

Job seekers often submit one-size-fits-all CVs that don't align with specific job requirements, miss ATS keywords, and lack the strategic prep needed to stand out in interviews. Existing tools force users to juggle multiple platforms—one for CV optimization, another for interview practice—missing the critical connection between tailoring and preparation.

Target Audience

Job seekers (fresh graduates, mid-career professionals, career switchers) and recruitment coaches seeking faster, smarter interview conversion

Personal Motivation

Empower candidates to present their best selves for every opportunity, reducing the friction between job search and interview success
