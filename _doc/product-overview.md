# CVTailor — Product Overview

## What It Is

CVTailor is an **AI-powered career tool** built around a single entry point: the job listing. Users paste a job description (or title + requirements), upload their current CV, and receive three fully tailored outputs in one intelligent workflow — a role-optimised CV, a structured interview prep plan, and an AI mock interview. It is the first **job-entry-first** platform to unify all three outputs in a single session.

## Core Problem

1. **Generic CVs** — most CVs are not aligned to the keywords, tone, or requirements an ATS or recruiter expects for a specific role.
2. **Unprepared candidates** — no structured, role-specific interview study plan; no practice with realistic questions.
3. **Siloed tools** — ATS optimisers don't connect to interview prep; mock-interview platforms don't start from your CV.

---

## Core Solution

One job entry unlocks everything:

1. **Upload CV + paste the job** — PDF or text CV; any job title, description, or full posting.
2. **AI analyses the gap** — maps the profile against the role, identifying keyword gaps, skill mismatches, and framing opportunities.
3. **Get three outputs instantly** — tailored CV, interview prep plan, mock interview session.

---

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Job Entry Analysis** | Extracts keywords, required skills, tone, and must-have criteria from any job listing — the foundation of every tailored output. |
| **CV Tailoring** | Rewrites and reprioritises the CV to match the role; ATS-ready; diff/highlight view shows every change. Export as PDF. |
| **Gap Analysis** | Shows exactly where the candidate's profile falls short versus the job, prioritised by impact. |
| **Interview Prep Plan** | Structured, role-specific roadmap: key topics, likely question categories, company research tips, and a study schedule. Downloadable PDF. |
| **Mock Interview** | AI asks role-relevant questions; candidate answers; receives instant scored feedback on content, structure, and delivery. |
| **Download-Ready Outputs** | Tailored CV and interview prep plan exportable as PDFs immediately. |

## Strategic Principles

1. **Job-entry-first** — every output is anchored to a specific role; no generic output is ever served.
2. **Unified workflow** — CV tailoring, gap analysis, prep plan, and mock interview live in one session, not separate products.
3. **Measurable outcomes** — all claims are metric-backed (speed, callback rate, ATS match, feedback latency).
4. **Privacy by default** — user CV data is not persisted without explicit consent.
